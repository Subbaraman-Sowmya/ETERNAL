# 🔱 Rigveda Pundit Roadmap (Ashvalayana Shaka)

This roadmap tracks the digital preservation and learning path for the 
**Rigveda Samhita** as applied through the **Ashvalayana Shrauta/Grihya Sutras**.

## 1. The Source: The 10 Mandalas (Samhita)
The goal is to map specific Suktas to their ritual applications.

| Mandala | Rishi / Family | Ritual Application (Ashvalayana) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **I** | Various (Medhatithi, etc.) | Standard Morning/Evening Agnihotra |
| **II** | Gritsamada | Specific Grita-related offerings |
| **III** | Vishvamitra | Gayatri Mantra & Savitri Upadesha |
| **IV** | Vamadeva | Agricultural and Protection Rites |
| **V** | Atri | Domestic Purification (Grihya) |
| **VI** | Bharadvaja | Administrative/Kshatriya Rites |
| **VII** | Vashistha | Varuna-related Shuddi Rites |
| **VIII** | Kanva/Angiras | General Sacrificial Framework |
| **IX** | Pavamana Soma | Soma Yaga (Shrauta) Procedures |
| **X** | Various (Vimada, etc.) | Vivaha (Marriage) & Antyesti (Final Rites) |
