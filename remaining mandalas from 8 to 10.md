The Remaining Mandalas (8, 9, & 10)
You have 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 7 already in the history. You are now in the "Home Stretch."

Mandala 8 (Kanva): The "Mixed" Mandala, very practical and diverse.

Mandala 9 (Soma): The "Purification" Mandala—dedicated entirely to the Soma juice (the nectar of clarity).

Mandala 10 (The Purusha): The "Final Architecture"—where the universe is described as a giant body (like a massive corporate or family structure).
